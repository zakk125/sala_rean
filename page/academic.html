This course is specially designed to fulfill the gap in the field of IT in Cambodia. Its main purpose is to train students specialize skills that are very important but lacking in today society in Cambodia. The areas of studies covered in this Master degree are: Oracle database administration modules, Oracle Application Development modules, Advance Computer Network Design and Implementations modules and last but no least Business Management modules.

Foundation Year Course

Foundation Year Course (FYC) program is a part of Bachelor degree course. Therefore, in order to attain a Bachelor degree, first year students have to complete FYC which is offered for one academic year.
